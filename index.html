What to do during containment ?

We are currently going through a difficult period in our history which is disrupting our daily lives and forcing us to rethink our ways and habits. Nevertheless, we must not lose heart! "What to do during containment" or WTDDC group accompanies you throughout your forties via their life coaches who enable you to continue to undertake and accomplish your projects.

The WTDDC experience.

1. Learn about you

"What to do during containment" is a platform created by two business school students whose aim is to offer personalised coaching to accompany you in your professional or personal administrative procedures. Through in-depth exchanges with your coach, the objective is to promote your personal development.

2. Topics of coaching session

Building your professional career : WTDDC supports you in the reflection concerning the choice of your professional voice, the potential further training to be carried out, interview simulations, participation in forums with the presence of partner companies.

Vocational retraining : e-learning courses and follow-up by the coach, reflection on the new professional path to take, work on oneself to develop cognitive skills.

Personal administrative project and preparation for retirement : support in all types of personal projects linked to a professional activity (e.g. investment in rental property), assessment of the working life, preparation of new voluntary or personal projects for profit or continuation of existing projects.
